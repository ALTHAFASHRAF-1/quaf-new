Thursday, July-25-2024 Programs & Reports

Freshers Meet

9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

By Darul Huda Islamic University, Chemmad

Tarhib 1446 Freshers' Grand Meet Up is a welcoming and celebratory event organized by the Centre for Public Education and Training (CPET) and the Office of Guidance and Extension Activities (OGEA) at Darul Huda Islamic University. Designed especially for freshers, the program marks the beginning of a new academic journey by fostering connection, inspiration, and a sense of belonging within the university community.

November, 2024 Programs & Reports

Website Launching

First Website for a class union in the history of Darul Hidaya Da'wa College, Manoor

QUAF launched an official website for its class union, marking a historic milestone as the first-ever website created for a class organization in the history of Darul Hidaya Da'wa College, Manoor.
The website was officially launched by the Vice Principal, Usthad Junaid Hudawi.

Career Development Program

March-1 to April-1

Ramadan Vocation
